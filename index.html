About Kemsley Construction

We are a family owned construction company specializing in remodels and ground-up construction. We've been proudly serving the Treasure Valley since 2021 with quality craftsmanship, honesty, and integrity.

Whether you are looking for new siding, a roof replacement, a shop build, or a complete home remodel, our team has the expertise to get the job done right. As a fully licensed and insured business, we bring a vision of modern excellence and structural integrity to every project.

Meet Mike

"I’m Mike, owner of Kemsley Construction. I find true satisfaction in taking on new challenges, giving my best to every project, and seeing it come to life after hours of hard work. There’s something special about seeing what a person can build with their own hands. As a family man, I care about my customers the same way I care about my own—treating every project with honesty, respect, and attention to detail."
